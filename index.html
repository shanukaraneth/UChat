What you get

Everything a chat app should be — nothing it shouldn't

UChat keeps the parts of social apps you love and drops the parts that waste your time.

Search first, chat instantly

Know someone's username? Just start talking. No waiting on friend requests — the other person decides if they want to stay connected or block you, any time.

Private, locked conversations

Add a personal password lock to any chat. It stays locked every time you close your browser, so no one else can casually open it on your device.

Share posts & quick flashes

Post photos and videos to your profile, and drop quick flashes that disappear after a day — perfect for the in-between moments.

Emojis & media, done right

A full emoji picker, and safe uploads for photos, videos and files — share what you mean, exactly how you mean it.

Stay logged in

Log in once and pick up right where you left off — you're only logged out when you choose to be.

Made to look like you

Switch between light and dark mode, and pick an accent colour that matches your vibe — it's your app, styled your way.

How it works

Up and running in under a minute

01

Create your account

Sign up with your email, your name and a unique username. Add a profile picture whenever you like.

02

Find someone by username

Search for anyone using the username they've shared with you, and open a chat right away.

03

Decide who stays close

Mark a chat as a Connection to keep them nearby, or Block anyone you'd rather not hear from — you can always change your mind later.

04

Chat your way

Send text, photos, videos and files, react with emojis, and lock any conversation with its own password for extra privacy.

Your conversations are yours.

Messages are encrypted before they ever leave your device's conversation, and access to them is restricted to the two people talking. Add a personal lock on top of any chat for a second layer of privacy that only you control.
